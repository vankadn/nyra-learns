🔴 Short Vowels
📣 Short vowels make a quick, soft sound — like a little hiccup!
A
cat 🐱
E
bed 🛏️
I
sit 🪑
O
hot 🔥
U
cup ☕
📌 Short vowels appear in closed syllables — a vowel followed by a consonant!

🐱 Short A — /ă/

cat map hand bat bag

🛏️ Short E — /ĕ/

bed ten leg red hen

🐟 Short I — /ĭ/

sit fish milk big win

🐶 Short O — /ŏ/

top dog sock hot box

☀️ Short U — /ŭ/

cup sun duck bug run
🟣 Long Vowels
🎵 Long vowels say their name — like when you say the alphabet!
A
cake 🎂
E
tree 🌳
I
bike 🚲
O
home 🏠
U
cute 🌸
✨ Magic E Rule: vowel + consonant + e = long vowel!
The silent E at the end makes the vowel long!
cap→cape
bit→bite
hop→hope
tub→tube

🎂 Long A — ā

cake name play rain day

🌳 Long E — ē

tree meet seed me she

🚲 Long I — ī

bike time five hi kite

🏠 Long O — ō

home boat road go snow

🌸 Long U — ū

cute music tube use
🔵 Vowel Teams
🤝 Two vowels work together to make one sound!
ai / ay
👉 Long A sound (ā)
rain train day play stay
ee / ea
👉 Long E sound (ē)
feet bee leaf meat seat
oa / ow
👉 Long O sound (ō)
boat soap snow grow blow
oo
👉 As in moon 🌙 or book 📚
moon spoon food book look
oi / oy
👉 "oi" sound
coin boil toy boy joy
ou / ow
👉 As in "ouch!" 😮
house mouse cloud cow town
au / aw
👉 "aw" sound
saw draw claw August
ei / eigh
👉 Long A sound
vein rein eight weight
